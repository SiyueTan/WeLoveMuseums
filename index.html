First, in this dataset, some museums' ranking and rate are overlaped. Let's looking at the data of top 100 museums.

Overall

From this visualization, We know the following facts:

No.1:Museums with higher rank are centrated between 4.5 to 5.0.

No.2:Museums with higher rating has more reviews.

No.3:Museums with higher review counts are centrated before rank 50.

Reveiw count and rank

This picture is very interesting. On the one hand, it proves that most museums with extremely high reviews are concentrated in the top five. On the other hand, the ranking of individual museums with high reviews is relatively low, which is related to the total number of museums in the country.

Rank and rating

Most of the high-ranking museums are focused on a score of 4.5. In the two areas of 4.5 and 5, there is a positive relationship between scoring and ranking. Some museums are evenly distributed in the area with a score of 4, but the number is not large, which is consistent with the picture "Over All". Therefore, in the area with a score of 4, ranking and score are not directly related. The areas 3 and 3.5 do not have regularity.
